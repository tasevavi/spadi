HOW CAN YOU HELP TODAY?



How to donate in 3 easy steps?

1.Log in your profile

2.Click Donate now

3.Add the items you want to donate and write a short description

Congratulations! You just made a small difference for someone who needs it!


Why donating textiles?

1.You help someone in a time of need

2.Instead of throwing away, you reuse items

3.You clean your space


What to donate for victims of floods?

1.Sanitation products

2.Medicine

3.Baby supplies

4.Gear to dig the mud


Where can you sign up for volunteering?

1.TimeHeroes

2.Local NGOs

3.CVS


How to determine if something is worth donating?

1.Is it clean, without stains or holes?

2.Is it still whole and good to wear?

3.Ask yourself - would you like to wear it or would you like your kid to wear it?

If all 3 answers are "yes", then you're good to go - donate now!
